OUR SERVICES

Farmland investment and agribusiness strategies

The demand for food in Africa and across the global outweighs the available supply. The situation is best addressed by increasing area under agriculture and increasing agriculture productivity by use of modern technology. However, the situation is an opportunity for investment in farmland, making Agrivest Solutions Limited to become an agribusiness investment vehicle that explores the farmland investment opportunities. The company makes direct investment on the farmland.

Farm business planning management services

ASL offers great services on farm planning and layout and business planning for its clients by using modern technologies. Through our farm management services we develop and manage agricultural/ farming assets which include farmland and farm operations conducted on it. This covers livestock and wide range of cropland farming with aim of realizing great returns from agriculture investment.

Agri-commodity trading

The company takes this opportunity to enable flow of commodities from the producers to end users. The company respects the contracts it has with the farmers and its buyers so as to ensure there is sustainability of the business and either party are thriving without compromising quality.

Consultancy services

Agrivest Solutions offers consulting services focusing on agribusiness investment, farmer centered capacity building and conducting surveys for institutions and individuals. We take pride in our great experience in the agriculture sector.

ACHIEVEMENT.

The company has been partnering with Youth for Green revolution, an NGO that provides a platform for young people to engage and practices agriculture at various entry levels along the agriculture value chains. The company mandate has been to offer high quality agribusiness training to young farmers and has successfully involved over sixty youths in fish farming business and poultry..
