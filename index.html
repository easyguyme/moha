Welcome

Agrivest Solutions Limited (ASL) is a registered limited liability company as per the Kenya’s company act that offers its target clientele high quality agribusiness and other related services..

Our Mission

To offer agribusiness solutions to farmers in terms of capacity building and business planning and production of high quality poultry, horticulture and animal feed products and develop new opportunities leveraging our expertise, resources with uncompromising dedication to efficiency, innovation, quality, service and talent development.

Our Vision

To be one of the best and inspiring agribusiness investment companies that excels in high quality service and product provision and reliable farmland investment vehicle.

  • We focus on professionalism

ACHIEVEMENT.

The company has been partnering with Youth for Green revolution, an NGO that provides a platform for young people to engage and practices agriculture at various entry levels along the agriculture value chains. The company mandate has been to offer high quality agribusiness training to young farmers and has successfully involved over sixty youths in fish farming business and poultry..
